# Phi Twin — Personal AI Agent

Phi Twin is a personal AI agent that runs entirely on your own computer.

No data leaves your machine. No cloud subscriptions. No third-party servers processing your information.

## What it does

You interact with it through a web dashboard on your phone or computer. It knows your professional context — your projects, your goals, your contacts — and helps you make decisions, track opportunities, and stay focused on what matters.

It can:

- Track job opportunities and score how well each one fits your profile
- Research companies and people you want to reach out to
- Keep a timeline of what you should be doing and why
- Ask you questions to fill in missing context before giving advice
- Manage a contact list with follow-up reminders

## How it works

It runs a local AI model (Phi-4) directly on your Mac. Everything stays local. You access it from any device on your private network.

## Why it exists

Most AI assistants send your data to external servers. This one does not. It is designed for people who want a capable personal assistant without giving up privacy.

## Tech used

Python · FastAPI · Ollama · Local LLM (Phi-4) · HTML/CSS/JS dashboard
